  • Jambo 1994

    75 Years scouting Ninove

    For the 75th anniversary of Scouts Ninove, we wanted to do something big. We aimed for the participation of half a dozen countries, Scouts Ninove being the only Belgian scouts and girlscouts, keeping balance between the various nationalities. In the end we would bid welcome to over 250 scouts members from different countries, among others there were Scots, Czechs, Germans, Italians, Dutchmen, … During this Jamborette we paid extra attention to recycling, there even was a ‘Greenteam’ present, setting a good example for other campers to handle all things correctly and sustainably.

  • Jambo 2010

    90 Years scouting Ninove

    In 2010, Scouts Ninove celebrated their 90th anniversary. For this reason another Jamborette was organized. Scouts from all over the world slipped down to Ninove, where meadows at Wolfsput were turned into a blooming scouts metropolis between July 24th and August 2nd. The theme was ‘Scouting in the Middle Ages’ en referred to the Middle Ages as much as possible. The different subcamps received names referring to known personalities from the Middle Ages (i.e. Camp Robin Hood, Bloody Mary, King Arthur, …). This second edition was a great success. Eventually, about 300 scouts members from 16 different nationalities took part in this unforgettable experience.

information

When?

The Jamborette takes place from saturday 18 to Tuesday, July 28, 2020..

What?

You’ll not get bored during this period! We have provided various activities such as games, shows, a big dinner etc..

Where?

Our third Jamborette will continue this year in the beautiful city of Ninove, "the heart of three pleasant regions" (Scheldeland, Flemish Ardennes and the region we call “The Green Belt”).

Ninove lies quite central in Belgium, so it’s not so far to cities like:

  • Aalst (15 km)
  • Gent (35 km)
  • Brussels (20 km)
  • Antwerp (70 km)

Our city lies on the banks of the Dender. This river arises in Aat and ends after 65 km in the Scheldt in Dendermonde.

Pricing

If you are interested or if there are any questions, do not hesitate to contact us. We are happy to give more explanation!
