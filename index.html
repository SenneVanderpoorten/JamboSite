  • Jambo 1994

    75 Years Scouting Ninove

    In 1994, the Boy Scouts from Ninove blew out 75 candles and they did not want to celebrate their anniversary alone. They decided to organize an international camp in their home town! The goal was to invite groups from six different countries and obtain a balanced mix of different nationalities. For that reason, the Boy and Girl Scouts from Ninove were the only Belgian groups present. Eventually, about 260 Scouts took part, coming from various countries like the Netherlands, Scotland, the Czech Republic, Germany and Italy!

    It was not ‘just’ an international camp because all the activities were built around one theme: recycling. To put this theme even more in the picture, a ‘Greenteam’ acted as role model for all the participants to use all the material in a sustainable and correct way. The camp was a great success and a sequel was inevitable …

  • Jambo 2010

    90 Years Scouting Ninove

    This sequel came in 2010, the year where the Boy Scouts of Ninove turned 90, which called for a second Jamborette. Scouting groups from all over the world came to Ninove, where the grasslands at the Wolfsput were transformed into a vibrant Scouting metropolis from the 24th of June until the 2nd of August 2010. The central theme of this camp became ‘Scouting in the middle ages’ and everything referred as much as possible to this time period. For example, the names of the subcamps referred to some well-known people from history and legends like Robin Hood, Bloody Mary and King Arthur. This second edition turned out to be a big success with about 300 Scouts from 16 countries attending this unforgettable event. Again, for the Boy and Girl scouts from Ninove, this tasted for more…

information

When?

The Jamborette takes place from saturday 18 to Tuesday, July 28, 2020..

What?

You’ll not get bored during this period! We have provided various activities such as games, shows, a big dinner etc..

Where?

Our third Jamborette will continue this year in the beautiful city of Ninove, "the heart of three pleasant regions" (Scheldeland, Flemish Ardennes and the region we call “The Green Belt”).

Ninove lies quite central in Belgium, so it’s not so far to cities like:

  • Aalst (15 km)
  • Gent (35 km)
  • Brussels (20 km)
  • Antwerp (70 km)

Our city lies on the banks of the Dender. This river arises in Aat and ends after 65 km in the Scheldt in Dendermonde.
