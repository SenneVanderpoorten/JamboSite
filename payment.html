Dear participants, citizens of Ninove and friends in scouting

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the global public health situation is developing rapidly on daily basis. Our Jamborette team is in close contact with the national autorities and local public institution to monitor the situation in Belgium and to measure all possibilities for our camp.

Our highest priority is the health, safety and well-being of all participants.

Our communication team is in close contact with all participating groups. Once again we would like to remind you that in case of any doubts, we are here to help you. As soon as we have any concrete information, we will publish our official communication on our website and facebook page. Our participants will ofcourse receive our official communications by mail as well.

We are keeping our fingers crossed and we hope you are all safe and sound.

Yours in scouting and stay safe!
Team Jamborette 2020
